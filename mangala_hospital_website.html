About Us

A Legacy of Trust & Medical Excellence

Located in the heart of Dharwad on SH 28, Tungabhadra HDS, Mangala Speciality Hospital is committed to providing accessible, high-quality healthcare services to every patient we serve.

Multi-Specialty Care

Covering 15+ medical disciplines under one roof for comprehensive treatment.

Modern Facilities

Equipped with advanced diagnostic and surgical technology for precise care.

24/7 Emergency Services

Round-the-clock emergency care for critical and urgent medical needs.

Patient-Centered Approach

Every treatment plan is tailored with the patient's well-being at the core.
